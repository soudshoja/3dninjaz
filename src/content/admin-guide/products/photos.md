# Product photos — good ones convert

A great photo makes the difference between a customer clicking "Add to bag" and leaving the page. Here's what works.

## How many photos to upload

Upload **3 to 5 photos** per product:
1. Main front view — clean background, well lit
2. Side or angle view — shows depth and detail
3. Scale reference — show it next to a hand, coin, or ruler so customers know the actual size
4. Detail close-up — texture, infill pattern, finish quality
5. Context shot — the product in use or on a shelf (optional but effective)

## Photo quality tips

- **Use natural light** — near a window on an overcast day gives soft, even light with no harsh shadows
- **Clean background** — white cardboard, a plain table, or a sheet works well
- **Steady camera** — use a phone tripod or prop your elbow on a table to avoid blur
- **Square crops** — the product card on listings is square (1:1). Crops that are too tall or wide get letterboxed

## Technical requirements

- Minimum width: 800px (1200px+ recommended)
- Format: JPG or PNG (the server converts to WebP automatically)
- File size: under 10 MB per photo (the server will compress it)

## Setting the thumbnail

The **first photo** is used as the thumbnail on product listing cards. To change which photo is the thumbnail:

1. Open the product for editing.
2. In the Photos section, click the star icon on the photo you want as thumbnail.

## Reordering photos

Drag photos left or right in the uploader to reorder them. The order you set here is the order customers see them in the photo gallery on the product page.

## Deleting a photo

Click the **X** button on any photo to remove it. Changes take effect when you click **Save product**.

**Tip:** Never delete all photos from an active product — a product with no photos won't display a thumbnail in the listing and will look broken to customers.
