# Email subscribers — exporting and managing

Customers and visitors can subscribe to your newsletter using the footer signup form on the storefront. All subscribers are managed from the **Subscribers** page.

## Viewing subscribers

Go to **Subscribers** in the sidebar (under Marketing). The page shows:
- Email address
- Subscription source (footer form, account creation, etc.)
- Status: Active, Unsubscribed, or Bounced
- Date subscribed
- Date unsubscribed (if applicable)

Use the filter tabs at the top to view Active, Unsubscribed, Bounced, or All subscribers.

## Exporting subscribers

To export your subscriber list to CSV:

1. Go to **Subscribers**.
2. Choose the filter you want (e.g., "Active" for your mailing list).
3. Click **Export CSV** (top right).
4. The CSV downloads with all subscriber data for that filter.

Use this CSV to import into Mailchimp, Brevo, or any email marketing tool.

## Unsubscribing someone manually

If a customer asks to be removed from your list but didn't use the unsubscribe link:

1. Find them in the Subscribers list.
2. Click the **Unsubscribe** button in their row.
3. Their status changes to Unsubscribed.

They will no longer receive newsletter emails. Transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates) are not affected — those are tied to orders, not subscriptions.

## Bounced subscribers

If an email address bounces (the email can't be delivered), the subscriber is automatically marked as "Bounced". These are excluded from future sends.

You don't need to manage bounces manually — they're handled automatically.

## Important note on bulk sending

The store's email system is designed for **transactional emails only** (order confirmations, shipping updates). There is no built-in bulk newsletter sending feature. To send newsletters, export the CSV and use a dedicated tool like Mailchimp or Brevo.

**Admin page:** `/admin/subscribers`
