# Printing shipping labels

After booking a courier via Delyva, the shipping label is available immediately.

## How to print a label

1. Open the order from **Orders** in the sidebar.
2. Scroll to the **Shipping** section.
3. After a successful booking, a **Print label** button appears.
4. Click it — the label opens as a PDF in a new browser tab.
5. Print it from your browser (File → Print, or Ctrl+P / Cmd+P).

## Label format

Delyva generates labels in A4 or A6 format depending on the courier. Most thermal label printers use A6 (100mm × 150mm). You can also print on plain A4 paper and fold it into the label sleeve on the parcel.

## What's on the label

- Sender address (your workshop address from Delyva settings)
- Recipient address (customer's delivery address)
- Consignment number and barcode
- Courier service name

## If the label doesn't open

- Check that your browser isn't blocking pop-ups for this site
- Try a different browser (Chrome works reliably)
- If the label URL has expired, refresh the order page and click Print label again — Delyva regenerates them on demand

## Sticking the label

- Use a clear label sleeve (available at stationery shops or on Shopee) to protect the label from moisture
- Make sure the barcode is flat and not folded — the courier scanner needs a clear read
- Place the label on the largest flat surface of the parcel

**Tip:** Print labels the night before your pickup window. Some couriers' morning pickups are very early (before 9am).
