# Booking couriers from an order

Once a customer has paid, you book the shipment directly from the order detail page.

## How to book

1. Go to **Orders** in the sidebar.
2. Click the order number to open it.
3. Scroll down to the **Shipping** section.
4. Click **Book courier**.
5. The system fetches live quotes from Delyva for the customer's postcode. You'll see a list of available couriers with prices and estimated delivery times.
6. Click **Book** next to your preferred courier.
7. Delyva confirms the booking. A **consignment number** and **tracking number** appear on the page.
8. The label PDF is available immediately — click **Print label** to open it.

## What happens after booking

- The order status automatically updates to "Shipped".
- The customer receives a shipping confirmation email with their tracking number.
- The tracking timeline on the customer's order page shows live status updates from Delyva.

## If no couriers appear

This means Delyva can't find services for that destination. Possible reasons:
- The customer's postcode is outside covered areas (e.g., rural Sabah/Sarawak)
- Your origin postcode in Delyva settings is a placeholder
- Delyva's API is temporarily down

In these cases, arrange the shipment manually and update the order status to "Shipped" yourself from the order detail page.

## Cancelling a booking

You can cancel a booked shipment as long as the courier hasn't picked it up. In the Shipping section of the order, click **Cancel shipment**. Note that Delyva may reject the cancellation if the courier is already en route — the error will show inline.

**Tip:** Book courier bookings early in the day. Most couriers have morning pickup windows. If you book after midday, the pickup may be the next working day.
