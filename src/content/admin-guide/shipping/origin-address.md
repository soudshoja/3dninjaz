# Setting your workshop address

Delyva uses your workshop (or home) address as the **pickup point** for all courier bookings. The address must be accurate — Delyva needs the postcode to calculate distances and determine which couriers serve your area.

## How to set it

1. Go to **Delyva courier** in the sidebar (under Operations).
2. Scroll to the **Origin address** section.
3. Fill in:
   - **Address line 1** — street address
   - **Address line 2** — unit number or floor (optional)
   - **City**
   - **State** — e.g., "Selangor"
   - **Postcode** — 5-digit Malaysian postcode
   - **Country** — Malaysia
4. Click **Save**.

## Why the postcode matters

The postcode is what Delyva uses to geocode your location. If the postcode is wrong or a placeholder (like `00000`), Delyva will return no courier quotes and customers will see no shipping options at checkout.

## Package defaults

Also in the Delyva settings, you can set default package dimensions and weight. These are used when a specific product variant doesn't have dimensions entered. Set them to a reasonable average — you can always adjust at booking time.

- **Default weight (kg)** — typical parcel weight for your products
- **Default dimensions (cm)** — length × width × height of your typical box

**Verify:** After saving, click **Test Connection** — it should show a green "Connected" status with your Delyva account name.
