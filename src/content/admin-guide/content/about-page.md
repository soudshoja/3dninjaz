# Updating About Us

The About Us page lives at `/about` on the storefront. It introduces your business to customers — who you are, why you started 3D Ninjaz, and what makes your products special.

## How to update it

The About page content is currently managed by editing the source code. It's not yet in the admin panel as an editable page.

To change the About page content, ask your developer to update the file at:
`src/app/(store)/about/page.tsx`

## What to include on your About page

A good About page for a small business covers:

- **Your story** — how you started 3D printing and why
- **What you make** — types of products, materials used
- **Quality commitment** — how you ensure products are well-finished
- **Location** — where you're based (Malaysia — which state/city)
- **Contact** — your WhatsApp number or email

## Making it personal

Customers buy from people they trust. A photo of you (or your printer in action) makes the page much more trustworthy than a stock image. Consider adding:
- A photo of your workspace
- A photo of products being packed
- A short video of a print in progress

## Social links on the About page

Social icons on the About page pull from the URLs you set in **Settings → Social & Contact**. Make sure those URLs are filled in — they appear at the bottom of the About page automatically.

**Storefront page:** `/about`
