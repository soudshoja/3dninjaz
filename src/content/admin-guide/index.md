# Welcome to the 3D Ninjaz Admin Guide

This guide covers every feature inside the admin panel — step by step, in plain language. You don't need to know anything about code or servers to use it.

## How to find what you need

Use the **search bar** at the top of this page to look up any topic by keyword (for example: "add product", "issue refund", "maintenance mode").

Or browse by category using the tiles below — each category groups related topics together.

## The admin panel at a glance

The sidebar on the left organises everything into groups:

- **Catalog** — Products, categories, and bulk import
- **Sales** — Orders and disputes
- **Finance** — Payments and reconciliation
- **Customers** — Customer accounts
- **Marketing** — Email templates, subscribers, coupons, reviews
- **Operations** — Shipping rates, Delyva courier, site settings

At the top of the sidebar you'll find the **Dashboard** — your daily summary of revenue, orders, and profit.

## Starting from scratch?

If you're setting up the store for the first time, go to the **[Launch Checklist](/admin/guide/launch)** first. It walks you through every setup step in the right order, from entering your business name to taking your first real payment.

## A note on saving changes

Every form in the admin panel has a **Save** button. Changes only take effect after you click Save — leaving a page without saving will discard your edits.
